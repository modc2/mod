# BlocTime Protocol: One-Page Overview

## What is BlocTime?

BlocTime is a revolutionary protocol that lets you **earn passive income from a thriving marketplace** - simply by locking up your tokens. Think of it as a savings account that pays you dividends from real business activity, not from printing new money.

## The Simple Idea

### For Token Holders (Stakers)
1. **Lock your tokens** for a period of time (measured in blockchain blocks)
2. **Receive BlocTime tokens** - the longer you lock, the more you get (up to 3x)
3. **Earn automatic rewards** from ALL marketplace fees - forever
4. **Claim anytime** without unstaking
5. **Get your tokens back** after the lock period ends

### For Marketplace Users
1. **Rent computing power, AI models, or digital assets** by the block
2. **Pay only for what you use** - transparent, fair pricing
3. **Resell unused time** on a secondary market if plans change
4. **Access premium resources** without huge upfront costs

## The Magic: Automatic Revenue Sharing

Every time someone uses the marketplace:
- **97.5%** goes to the resource owner (fair compensation)
- **2.5%** goes to a treasury that automatically distributes to ALL stakers
